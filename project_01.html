Ball Movement

The ball is a flat 2d object brought to life with an oval shadow under it. The ball and shadow are connected to a null object in their movement and the ball rotation is separate. I calculated the distance and rotation of the ball so it wasn’t gliding on the floor.

Stanley Jump

The bottle animation was more complex. The legs bend and compress before he jumps which is the same distance it squats. I used the warp tool to achieve this. The shadow is aligned on the same key frames of his movements. I used a simple scale effect to do this.

Floating

The floating was done with a wiggle effect to add movement. The teardrop coming from the eye was linked to the object. It slowly falls by the side until it hits the water. The droplet is faded out from the top. As soon as it hits the water it is then its separate layer and effect of an oval stroke that fades its path to create a splash effect.

Fish

The fish were simple in their movements. I eased them with a long stop like they were still floating in liquid. As they aren’t the focus a simple flip done with the scale effect was all that was needed for their turning animation.
